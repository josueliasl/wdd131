Sumary

The site plan aims to showcase the jewelry collection, define the target audience, and create a user-friendly platform that drives sales and builds brand trust.

Site Name

Golden Petals

"Golden Petals" embodies elegance and craftsmanship, combining timeless beauty with delicate, nature-inspired artistry to create a memorable brand identity focused on high-quality jewelry.

Optional domain:
GoldenPetalsJewelry.com

Site Purpose

The website will feature our jewelry collection with a user-friendly experience, including product details, images, secure payments, and reviews to enhance the brand and boost sales..

Scenarios

1.Browsing and Discovery

- Users explore jewelry categories with filters for category.

2. Product Purchase

 - Customers easily select a product and complete a simple, secure checkout process.

5. Mobile Shopping

 - The site is optimized for mobile, offering fast loading times, an intuitive interface.

Color Schema

The fonts—Playfair Display (#FFD700), Lora (#FFFFFF), and Cinzel (#333333)—will create an elegant, luxurious look, perfectly complementing the color scheme to highlight the high-end jewelry design.

Typography

Using **Cinzel Decorative** for headings and **Cinzel** for body text creates contrast while maintaining consistency. This enhances elegance and readability, balancing luxury and user experience.
